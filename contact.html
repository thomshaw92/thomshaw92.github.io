Recent Coverage

Scanning the tongue to detect and track Motor Neurone Disease

October 2025

Research showing that standard MRI scans of the tongue can detect and track MND received widespread international coverage. People living with MND who have difficulty speaking or swallowing tend to have smaller tongue muscles, with lower tongue volume associated with worse prognosis.
