About this Atlas

This interactive visualisation shows the HCP-MMP1.0 cortical parcellation (Glasser et al., 2016) mapped onto the left hemisphere of the fsaverage template brain. The atlas divides the cortex into 180 distinct regions per hemisphere based on multi-modal MRI data including cortical architecture, function, connectivity, and topography.

Toggle between the folded (pial) surface showing the brain's natural gyri and sulci, and the inflated surface which reveals regions hidden within sulcal folds. Black boundary lines delineate each parcellation region.
