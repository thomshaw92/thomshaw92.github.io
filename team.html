Available Projects

Multimodal MRI Biomarkers for Motor Neuron Disease

This project will develop and evaluate multimodal MRI biomarkers for the diagnosis and monitoring of MND. The successful candidate will integrate imaging measures including structural, diffusion, functional, spectroscopic, and spinal MRI into computational models that capture disease heterogeneity.

Interested candidates should contact t.shaw@uq.edu.au.
